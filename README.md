# Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC) - Seminar Project

## 📌 Overview
This repository contains my seminar presentation on **Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC)**, completed as part of my **B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)** at **VIT Vellore**.

The seminar focuses on the working principle, components, advantages, applications, and the role of SSSC in improving power transmission efficiency and stability in modern electrical power systems.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand the concept of Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC)
- Study its working principle
- Explore its applications in power systems
- Analyze its advantages over conventional compensation methods
- Understand its significance in HVDC and FACTS technology

---

## 📚 Topics Covered
- Introduction to SSSC
- Need for Series Compensation
- Working Principle
- Construction and Components
- Operating Modes
- Advantages
- Applications
- SSSC in HVDC Transmission
- Conclusion
